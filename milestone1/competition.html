Competition

What the competition is doing right.

Techincally, nothing. As far as I've searched, I haven't found a website that does what this website will do. However, Satisfactory Wiki and Satisfactory Calculator combined can allow you to more easily plan your own factories.

What these websites are doing well is

  • Keeping information up to date
  • Linking to internal sources for information on terminology and other info
  • Following a similar visual style to the game itself

What the competition is doing wrong.

The websites listed before are

  • Not allowing design models to be uploaded
  • Not keeping text organized across the navigation bar and other items are messy

How I can do it better.

I will

  • Use the same themes similar to the game, but keep everything organized and intuitive
  • Upload direct model diagrams onto the website, not blueprints or a clunky calculator (Satisfactory Modeler can handle that)
