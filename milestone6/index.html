About

Satisfactory Designs is a website built to serve users a simple way to view the most efficent setups to produce items in the video game Satisfactory. Across the site, there are different pages with information on building/logistics methods, software to make the process easier, and the designs (using Satisfactory Modeler) that will help you create the most efficient factory.

Satisfactory

Satisfactory is a first-person open-world factory building game with a dash of exploration and combat. Play alone or with friends, explore an alien planet, create multi-story factories, and enter conveyor belt heaven!

Description from Steam page

Usage

This site is not intended to be used as replacement for your own creativity. This is why there are no downloadable blueprints. The sole purpose of this site is to help alleviate the stress caused by the math and planning required to make your factory efficient. While this won't eliminate it, Satisfactory Designs will help reduce mistakes in the planning process and streamline the growth of your factories.

Getting Started

To start, look through the Methods pages and see the building and logistics techniques that will make the whole experience more convenient. Then, wherever you are in the game, find recipes for that section and start reviewing the designs there. Also, you are highly encouraged to go ahead and use Satisfactory Modeler yourself to make your own systems fitting your needs directly.
